👔 Board of Directors

The Alaska Fisher House Board of Directors is comprised of dedicated community members who are deeply committed to the goal of making Fisher House in Alaska a reality. Recognizing the critical need for affordable housing for our military families during times of medical need, the board directs their efforts toward raising funds and providing support for the ongoing mission of this important service.
