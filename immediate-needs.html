💝 Immediate Needs & How You Can Help

The Alaska Fisher House relies on generous donations from individuals and organizations to support our military families during their time of need. Your contribution directly helps us provide essential items our guests require during their stay.

💰 Make a Monetary Donation

Drop Off in Person

Cash, checks, or gift cards can be dropped off at our location during business hours.

Mon-Fri: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Donate Online

Credit card donations available online through our secure donation portal.

🛒 Items We Need

Food & Beverage Items

📦 Pantry Staples

  • Bottled water
  • Shelf-stable whole & 2% milk
  • Canned soup
  • Cereal
  • Individually wrapped snacks:
    • Crackers
    • Chips
    • Cookies
    • Granola bars
    • Slim Jims
    • Jerky

⚠️ Note: We cannot accept opened or expired food items.

Personal Care & Hygiene

🧴 Toiletries & Personal Care

  • Travel-sized toiletries
  • Q-tips
  • Deodorant
  • Mouthwash
  • Dental floss
  • Hair brushes and combs
  • Hair ties and bobby pins
  • Chapstick
  • Tweezers
  • Hair spray
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Nail clippers
  • Toothpaste

Household & Cleaning Supplies

🧹 Cleaning & Household Items

  • Toilet paper
  • Paper towels
  • Paper bowls and plates
  • Disposable coffee cups with lids
  • Kleenex
  • 13 gallon kitchen trash bags (drawstring)
  • Dishwasher tabs
  • Liquid dish soap
  • Laundry detergent (liquid for HE front load)
  • Color safe bleach
  • Swiffer Wet Jet pads and liquid
  • Ziploc bags (various sizes)
  • Tupperware
  • Aluminum foil
  • Plastic wrap

🎁 Miscellaneous Items

  • Umbrellas
